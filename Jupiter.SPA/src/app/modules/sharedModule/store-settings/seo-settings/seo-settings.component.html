Meta Title
Title tags are used to tell search engines and visitors what your website is about in the most concise and accurate way possible. This title will show up as a big blue link in search engine results.
Meta Description
The meta description is a short paragraph of text placed in the HTML of a webpage that describes its content. The meta description will appear under your page’s URL in search results. This is also known as a snippet.
